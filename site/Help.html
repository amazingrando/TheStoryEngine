The Story Engine Deck & Deck of Worlds

Create endless story ideas.

The Story Engine Deck & Deck of Worlds are decks of almost 1600 cards you can combine to create story ideas, creative writing prompts, and D&D campaign ideas.

Learn How It Works

How to use this application:

Adding a card

On the left side there is a list of all the decks that are available,
press one of the icons to add a card from that type to the canvas.

Moving a card

Cards that are on the canvas can be moved with the mouse.
Click and hold a card to drag it to it's new location, then release the mouse button.

Rotate a card

Cards that are on the canvas can be selected by clicking on them with the mouse.
Selected cards can be rotated by pressing the left or right arrow keys.
Some cards can be rotated to 4 directions, others only 2 and some not at all, the software knows this and will rotate the card accordingly.

Change order

Cards that are on the canvas can be selected by clicking on them with the mouse.
The level of the selected card can be changed by pressing the up and down keys.
The up key will bring the card to the front, the back key will send the card to the back.
Since all cards are in a single order, you might have to press the up or down button several times to bring the selected card to the desired level.
